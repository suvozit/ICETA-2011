Welcome!

Hi and welcome to Ending Credits! My name is Karthik Raveendran and I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, advised by Dr. Greg Turk. I'm particularly interested in physically based simulation and animation, augmented reality and game design.

I joined the PhD program in 2009 after completing my Masters in Computer Science at Georgia Tech, where I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant (GRA) with Dr. Blair MacIntyre at the Augmented Environments Lab. Prior to that, I graduated from Nanyang Technological Univeristy (NTU), Singapore with a Bachelors in Computer Engineering. My hometown is Bangalore, India.

In case you are wondering, Ending Credits happens to be a song by Opeth, one of my favourite bands. Also, I think it's a lot easier to type than my full name :)
